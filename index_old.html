Roadmap

Investors usually like startups with strong teams, so I hope the Hubcoin will be able to get them interested. For example, bloomberg article

1-10 June

On June 1-10 I will be searching for developers who may be interested and offering them to join us and to get a portion of premine. I am not going to force them to work as I believe that a portion of premine itself will serve a sufficient motivation. To join the team, developer only has to write a reply and promise to receive share when we launch the coin.

10-31 June

On June 10-31, while waiting for replies from developers, I will be making a website (as I am a web programmer myself) and promoting the coin. When the team is basically ready, I will start searching for investors, offering them to join us at the next stage when the coin appears at an exchanger or when we start ICO.

1 July

On July 1 I will make coinmarketcap.com screenshots, I will also distribute premine between the developers and save those data.







Publish

Together we will choose the further goals of the coin and make our future plans. I think the mission of the Hubcoin should consist in uniting altcoin developers, creating instruments for them, and providing assistance with investments and promotion.

Features

Premine size - 10%

First Hubcoin goal is to create extremly strong development team. Premine will be distributed between all altcoin developers.

Capitalization

7% of the coins will be distributed between the developers of the altcoins (or assets/tokens) already existing at one of the exchangers, in proportion to their capitalization.

Distributed

2% of the coins will be equally distributed between all the altcoin developers who have a wallet, a block explorer, and a topic at this forum.

Promotion

1% of the coins is my premine. I have a few bitcoins for the project development, so I am not going to spend it until the coin reaches high capitalization.

Nem

This is the key Hubcoin idea, inspired by NEM (2 biilion capitalization for now).NEM has proven that experiments with fair coins distribution have great potential.

Idea

While NEM distributes premine between all comers, we only give it to other coin developers. Any developer of altcoins is much more skilled in their improvement and promotion than an ordinary user.
